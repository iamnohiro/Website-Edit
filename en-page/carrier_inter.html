Carrier Interconnection

By partnering with Parlacom, carriers can provide complete end-to-end M2M service. This allows customers to achieve real-time connectivity and instant interaction between applications and devices over a secure wireless communication infrastructure.

Our M2M application platform allows carriers to provide high value application layer M2M services. This results in two distinctive advantages: Greater average revenue per device and expanded cloud-based service offerings.

An added benefit is that our personnel have extensive knowledge of the inner workings of the carrier networks, data centers and Parlacom M2M technology. Acting as an interface, our technicians aid in the quick setup and commissioning of the infrastructure.

Carrier Interconnection Service Includes:

  • VPN connectivity with mobile carriers supporting L2TP, GRE and IPSec.
  • Provision for a minimum of two fault-tolerant connections to the carrier's network operations center.
  • 24/7 technical support.
  • Ability to detect if a device is active on a carrier's network.
  • Highest degree of security to protect customer privacy. Only Parlacom clientele can traverse the connections.
  • Built-in security systems using AES256 IPSec standards based encryption.

Benefits To Mobile Carriers Include:

  • Private IP. Save on limited public IP space.
  • Safeguard revenue by eliminating millions in unpaid bills.
  • Prevent fraud by eliminating unwanted traffic.
  • Improve overall network security and performance.
  • Optimize bandwidth usage.
