COMPANY PROFILE

Parlacom, a leading M2M company in Brazil, is changing the way the world communicates. With our cutting edge machine-to-machine (M2M) technology, you will enjoy faster data and a fully secure network to operate your business, connect and manage your M2M devices

As one of the largest M2M operators in Brazil, Parlacom manages over 200 thousand M2M devices and SIM cards; over 40 Terabytes of data and 100 million connections every month. Our portifolio of customers include more than 10 thousand small, medium and large companies all over Brazil. Running in two heavy-weight Data Centers, more than 30 dedicated and high performance servers provide a smooth 24x7 operation for mission-critical applications.

Parlacom offers a wide range of M2M services including data, messaging, and device management, backed by a full suite of APIs for complete integration with your backoffice systems. You can get instant access to your Parlacom M2M platform with our managed data center, Android and Iphone applications.

By giving you open access to your own M2M control panel, you have the power to monitor your data usage in real time and manage SIM cards with the click of a mouse. That means your business can streamline supply chains, manage fleets, and monitor spending. Ultimately, we make it easy to meet customer demands and keep your overhead cost low.

Our team of engineers has over 20 years of experience and has served in 15+ countries around the world. Parlacom is a leading telecommunications and technology giant in Brazil. With the ability to handle asset management, xfleet tracking, payment processing, and remote security monitoring, many growing companies are choosing Parlacom to manage their machine-to-machine needs.

Customer service is the number one goal of Parlacom and we are proud to offer 24/7 support for any issues that you might be facing.
