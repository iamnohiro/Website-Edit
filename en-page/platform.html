Our Platform

Parlacom's platform and system has been custom designed for the highly unique and specialized needs of M2M service providers. Our platform is reliable, flexible, scalable, and it is operated in real time.

We currently offer service to more than 10,000 activation requests every day and we have the capacity to manage over 200 billion records on a monthly basis. Our engineers have designed the platform to scale up in order to handle over 10 million devices.

The Parlacom platform is directly connected to the mobile carrier using advanced VPN connectivity. This ensures that all activations performed by clients are accurately reflected on their device in real time.

Every platform in the Parlacom system is completely separate and redundant with fail-over capabilities. Our M2M platform allows for seamless integration of additional remote machines, M2M devices, and business systems. The benefits of choosing Parlacom include:


  • Reduced MTTR (Mean Time to Repair) – Visibility of network performance. Real time alerts with specifics. Dispatch the right personnel with the right parts. Less down time.
  • Instant alerts on performance degradation allows for the opportunity to fix issues before they result in machine failure.
  • Improved maintenance – Strategy that works on predictive maintenance rather then reactive maintenance. Monitor and repair issues before in impacts the customer.
  • Optimized parts inventory – Free your working capitol by streamlining your stock purchases.
  • Automated audit trail – Drastically reduce human error and endless paper trails with automated data collection.
  • Smart business service – When you connect with Parlacom we manage every aspect of the downstream connectivity for both revenue generating traffic flow and business systems interfaces.
  • Create new service offerings that generate revenue based on real time data.
