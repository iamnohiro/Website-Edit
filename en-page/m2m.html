M2M Data

Have you ever received a phone bill and felt instantly sick when you saw unexpected data charges? Most customers don't recognize the high data traffic they accrue each month until they get a bill so high that they simply cannot pay it.

Why Should You Choose Parlacom's Data Platform? Parlacom is based in two of the largest US data centers, so you can enjoy unlimited data each month with no worry about slow speeds or dropped coverage.

Instant Data Management Parlacom offers you a two-way channel to monitor and collect data from the remote M2M devices that you have deployed. You can use the two-way channel to:

  • Collect product and usage information.
  • Send instructions or software updates, or remotely monitor the device.

Customers can manage all aspects of their devices using a single platform with APIs to integrate the data from these devices back into their servers. We do all the work for you to simplify the complexity of deploying and managing a multi-network M2M solution.

The Most Robust Network The Parlacom network has been designed with a high degree of fault tolerance and self-recovery from disasters. Our network is already prepared to accommodate another 10 million devices.

Top Rated Security M2M 3G VPN is the ideal package for customers of Telemetry, Tracking, Asset Security, and POS systems". The VPN tunnels managed by Parlacom establish data communication between mobile operators and customers, making the data traffic flow in a controlled, secure manner via VPN (virtual private network). The platform offers complete control of SIM card management and VPN connections for every 3G handset, traffic control, monitoring and management in real time.
