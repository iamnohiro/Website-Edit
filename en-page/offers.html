Parlacom's Unique Offering

Our technology relies on 100% web based applications. When you choose us for your M2M needs, you receive secure transactions to safeguard your data. Our cloud-based technology platform makes it possible for businesses to enjoy M2M benefits with no equipment or infrastructure on the customer's site.

We support carriers and customers around the world. Our highly skilled engineers come from centers of excellence in the United States, England, China, India, and Brazil.

We have a strong footprint in Brazil with dedicated connectivity with all carriers. Presently, we have more than 200,000 devices in operation throughout Brazil and our technology is servicing thousands of companies, ranging from small business to large corporations. Our M2M Platform provides:

  • Private APN and M2M platform
  • Private M2M corporate Intranet
  • Private, fixed IP addresses
  • Mobile device management platform for SIM cards
  • Bandwidth control and optimization
  • Traffic control via firewalls
  • Call detail record and reporting
  • Revenue assurance
  • Fraud detection and prevention
  • Universally compatible with all carriers. One stop shop for your M2M services
  • Connectivity with Brazilian and international carriers
  • Robust and proven Platform, one of the largest in the country, supporting over 200k simcards and thousands of customers
  • Our technology is based in two of the largest US Data Centers, fully equipped with 11,000 square meters of installation and capacity for 40,000 servers. We provide more than 30 gigabits of connectivity for internet access and we can support thousands of terabytes worth of data each month without compromising speed, security, or performance.


Industry Leading Technology Assigned Exclusively For M2M Quality and Service Parlacom's industry best M2M 3G VPN is ideal for telemetry, tracking, asset security, and POS systems. Our VPN technology helps your business establish communication between operators and customers with a controlled and secure VPN (virtual private network). Our platform offers complete, real time control for 3G handsets, traffic control, and monitoring and management via the web portal.


Based in two of the largest U.S. Data Centers, offering:

  • Capacity for 40,000 servers.
  • 60+ gigabytes of connectivity supporting thousands of terabytes of data every month, without the loss of performance.
  • Uninterrupted service provided by generators and backup power supply. 24/7 services.
  • Fully secure monitoring and access control.
  • Dual data centers help provide full redundancy for critical applications.
