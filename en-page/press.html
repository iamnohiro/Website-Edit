PRESS ROOM

2014 MAY 06

Parlacom Expanding M2M Services in Brazil

Brazil, being one of the largest countries in the world by population has a very significant demand for Machine-to-machine services. However, the country's regulatory environment's high level of protection has presented significant barriers to both new and seasoned players in the market.

MAY 08

Tax Regulation Opens More Doors for M2M Business in Brazil

The tax reduction regulation for M2M services is finally taking effect in March of next year as confirmed by Diário Oficial da União, Brazil's federal gazette. The regulation targets to bring down installation and operation inspection fees that are paid by operators for all M2M terminals as part of the telecommunications fund.
