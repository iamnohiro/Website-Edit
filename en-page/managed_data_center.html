Managed Data Center

Our Managed Data Center is an important resource for businesses that rely on technology for daily operations. We keep your data readily available and fully secure. With our storage services, you can reduce costs and rest in peace that your data is always backed up and fully protected. We Take Security Seriously When your business is on the line, there is no room for hackers or computer failure. These can cost you thousands of dollars and weeks of productivity. That's why Parlacom offers a Managed Data Center to back up your files and insure that all of your vital information is secure and instantly available through our unique cloud platform. We Free Up Your Resources Our Managed Data Center allows you to eliminate local servers and run all operations through one central data center. This consolidates all of your technology to one simple platform and gives you easy access to manage devices and reduce overall computing costs. We free up your resources to focus on the most important thing – your customers – while we handle updates, data security, and compilation. Avoid Costly Interruptions If you business relies on connectivity, then one failed device can bring all operations to a screeching halt. At Parlacom, we help you prevent costly interruptions by backing up all of your data through our platform. That means you never lose important files and you have total peace of mind that all mission critical functions are sustainable and secure.
