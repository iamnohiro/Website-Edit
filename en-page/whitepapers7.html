Whitepaper: Leveraging Parlacom's Managed Data Center

A Managed Data Center is a facility used to house computer systems and associated components. These systems may include telecommunication and storage equipment as well as redundancy devices, backup power supplies, back up data connections, environmental controls and applications. Meanwhile, Managed Data Services are technology related facilities, components and activities supporting projects and operations of a managed data center and generally comes in two categories: services provide for a data center and services provided to a data center.

IT operations are always very, if not the most crucial aspect of an organization's operations. One of company's major concerns is ensuring business continuity as they largely rely on their systems. Data centers have undergone several transformations before companies are able to enjoy the benefits of the modern-day Data Center. And the Telecommunication Infrastructure Standard for Data Centers strictly specifies requirements of these centers, including single tenant enterprise data centers and multiple tenant internet hosting centers.

Market Overview: Managed Data Center

The Managed Data Center Services market has greatly gained importance in more recent years, primarily due to the adoption of cloud-based platforms and the successful deployment of cloud-based services. Today, Small to Medium-size Enterprises are manifesting great interest in the outsourcing of their IT services to third-party vendors.

While cost and service levels are still indispensable IT segments, guaranteeing that the outsourced services are perfected to convey the right business quality is currently a top priority. Organizations are turning to managed data services in building their profit and to bring down expenses for their non-essential IT solutions, which enables them to concentrate on more significant initiatives. As SMBs transition to the use of Data Centers, this market is expected to grow considerably.

The North American market became the largest market in managed data services, which accounted to 35.08% of overall volume. Together with Europe, it has accounted to 63% of the global market in 2013.

Forecast from Market Research shows that:
"Managed Data Center Services will have a 21.4% CAGR in a five year period, from 2013 through 2018."
