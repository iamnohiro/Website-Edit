Our Clients

Great communication is vital to all companies. At Parlacom, we understand that your business needs practical solutions to enhance the communication within your organizational network. In an effort to better serve our clients, Parlacom invests in developing cutting edge technology for your mobile technology needs.

The leading M2M companies consistently choose our M2M technology as the basis for their operational services and support needs. Areas that we serve include:

  • Public Transportation
  • Payment Systems using Point of Sale Terminals
  • GPS vehicle tracking companies
  • Natural gas utility companies
  • Security and asset protection firms
  • Sales force automation
  • Smart Grids for Utility Companies
  • E-Health
  • SIM Card Management

When you choose Parlacom, you will enjoy several major advantages.

  • Our cloud-based platform requires no special equipment or infrastructure for you, the customer.
  • 100% web-based management applications, fully customizable and easy to integrate.
  • Supports carriers and customers anywhere in the world. This includes L2TP, IPSec, and GRE VPN tunnels.
  • Highly skilled technicians in the United States, England, India, China, and Brazil.
  • Live, 24/7 technical support
  • 20+ years of experience in the US and Brazilian market in MVNO's and telecom companies.
  • Dedicated connectivity with major carriers in Brazil such as Telefonica/Vivo, CLARO, OI, and TIM.
  • 200,000+ M2M devices in full operation serving thousands of small/mid/large companies.
  • VPN connectivity with payment clearinghouses and corporate intranets.
  • 100 million+ connections every month; 40+ Terabytes of inbound/outbound traffic
